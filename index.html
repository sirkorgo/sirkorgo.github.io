Meet the Characters!

Sirkorgo

Meet sirkorgo! He's an energetic, a leader, and most importantly, can shapeshift! Sirkorgo loves researching and building little gadgets! If sirkorgo were to be compared to someone from the Sonic universe, he'd be a lot like Tails!


Widget

Check out Widget! Widget is an experimental Artifical Quantum Lifeform (AQL) in the shape of a UFO! He's a quick thinker, pays attention to small details, and can fly really fast with his built in motor. Widget and Sirkorgo are best buds and are basically inseparable.


Mono

Say hi to Mono! Mono is Sirkorgo's little brother, and a remarkable young boy! Mono is a lot smarter than the average middle schooler, so he gets to skip class and tag along with Sirkorgo and Widget occasionally! He may be a bit shy, but is still very playful! Despite not having arms or legs, Mono is able to get around pretty much anywhere by bouncing, rolling, and when needed, by DIGGING in the ground!
